Ariyan Paul is a fine art wildlife photographer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

“In every walk with nature, one receives far more than he seeks.” — John Muir

My photography focuses on quiet natural moments — city birds, rooftop wildlife, moonlit skies, and the small details of life that often go unnoticed.

Featured Shot

These photographs explore the beauty of birds living beside us — on rooftops, solar panels, open concrete spaces, and city corners filled with light and shadow.
