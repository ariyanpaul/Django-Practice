About Ariyan Paul

My Story

Capturing wildlife with patience, respect, and curiosity.

Ariyan Paul is an experimental fine art wildlife photographer from Dhaka, Bangladesh. His work focuses on the emotional connection between humans and the natural world, blending documentary-style observation with artistic composition.

Ariyan began photography during simple rooftop and nature walks, where he became fascinated by birds, city wildlife, changing skies, and quiet outdoor moments.

His portfolio includes bird photography, urban wildlife, landscape studies, night sky frames, and close-up natural textures. Ariyan believes every photograph should encourage people to slow down and appreciate the beauty already around them.
